UI design

The design for Digitalist agency focuses on the main thing - context, visually emphasizing cards and information. The layout is built on a 12-column grid, which provides logical and convenient navigation.

  • A dark theme with green accents, in accordance with the client's preferences.
  • Information blocks are designed as glass-effect cards, emphasizing transparency and clarity while structuring key data on the site
  • Colorful Tetris-style shapes symbolize the ease and structure of project work, fostering a friendly vibe and softening the formal business style.
  • IMB Graphik conveys technological sophistication and compliance with modern IT standards
  • Nunito adds friendliness and flexibility, reflecting a comfortable collaboration with clients
