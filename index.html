about me

I’m a designer, and two things matter most to me: responsibility and creativity. My goal isn’t just to make things look good but to create designs that truly work for businesses—attracting customers, increasing conversions, and making products more user-friendly and appealing.
Design isn’t just about visuals; it’s a tool for business growth.
I see it as a bridge between an idea and its realization, where every element has meaning and brings value.

Design isn’t just about “making it look pretty”— it’s a full-on investigation! First, I ask the right questions, dig deep into user and business needs, and only then i do start working on layouts. For me, every project isn’t just something that exists—it has to actually work. From the first “What do we need here?” to the final “Oh, now it’s easy!”.
I focus on results that make life simpler (and keep users from losing their minds)

My last name is Kvitka —
that means flower in Ukrainian :)
