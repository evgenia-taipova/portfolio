FREsH BLACK

Fresh Black offers a range of coffee-related products and services: selling high-quality coffee beans, selling the Morning capsule coffee machine, renting coffee machines for cafés and other businesses, providing maintenance and repair services for coffee machines, operating a barista school where professionals learn to work with coffee. Most importantly, introducing people to real, high-quality coffee beans.
In this project I worked to create a user-friendly e-commerce site.

Timeline 1 month, 2024

UX DESIGN | UI DESIGN | REDESIG | RESEARCH

Customer problems
and needs

High user drop-off from the homepage and product pages. A user flow that caters to both specialty coffee enthusiasts and newcomers who are curious but not yet fully engaged. A checkout experience that is both overwhelming and lacking in essential information.

Main objectives of the project
  • Conduct 7+ interviews with existing Fresh Black customers from each team
  • Identify and document user personas, detailing their needs and motivations regarding coffee as a product (Why coffee?)
  • Identify and document usability issues on the current website based on real user feedback
  • Identify and document usability issues on the current website based on real user feedback
  • Outline improvement suggestions for the site's structure, incorporating best practices and insights from competitors and European market leaders
  • Create a Figma prototype (specify the level of detail: wireframe, mid-fidelity, high-fidelity) with proposed solutions to enhance the user experience

Stages of work

To complete the design within the agreed timeline, I break the project into clear stages. I also allocate an additional 3 days for revisions, if needed.

analysis of the existing website

After a detailed analysis of the existing Fresh Black website, I identified several areas that needed improvement. I reviewed the homepage and navigation, product pages, checkout process, shopping cart, and the mobile version of the site. Additionally, I assessed and documented how easy it was to place an order through the website.

Competitor analysis

I personally analyzed 5 Ukrainian competitor websites and 5 European ones.

I also wrote a summary of the competitive analysis to identify key opportunities for improvement and potential challenges for each competitor, as well as for the Fresh Black website itself.

Interviews with real customers

The main goal of the in-depth user interviews was to understand the experience of Fresh Black Coffee website users, identify their pain points and needs, and uncover the key factors influencing their coffee purchasing decisions.

  • Research questions were formulated
  • Interview script was written
  • Four in-depth qualitative interviews were conducted
  • Personalized messages for respondent outreach via messengers were prepared
  • Each interview was transcribed
  • Analysis and key insights from the client interviews were summarized

SolutionS presentation

In the presentation for the CEO of Frech Black Coffee I presented the research findings, clearly outlined the identified issues and justified the UX solutions that would help improve conversion and user engagement

results

Recommendations are based on real data, which will significantly improve the usability of the site and, as a result, increase the number of successful transactions. These solutions are aimed at increasing customer satisfaction and optimizing the purchasing process

    During the research, my team and I were focused on analyzing the current user experience on the Frech Black Coffee website. We identified several key issues impacting conversion and user engagement. We proposed targeted UX solutions to optimize the core processes:

  • Optimizing the order process: simplifying the purchase steps will allow users to complete their orders faster and easier, contributing to increased conversion rates
  • Improving the catalog structure: we recommended updating the navigation and product categories for easier search and better product visibility, enabling users to find what they need more efficiently
  • Enhancing the user interface convenience: based on the real needs of customers, we developed changes that reduce user errors and improve overall site interaction
